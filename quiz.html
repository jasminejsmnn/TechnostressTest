Technostress Test

1. I feel reliant on technology and gadgets to complete simple daily tasks

2. I feel the need to constantly stay connected and within reach to my acquaintances through mobile apps

3. I feel pressured to constantly keep up with the emergence of new technological devices, apps and sites

4. I worry that my lack of technological abilities will limit me from securing a job position in the future

5. I believe that the high amount of information online is overwhelming and make me confused at times

6. I feel irritable towards my friends, families and acquaintances due to overhwelming use of technological devices

7. I often have trouble sleeping because of the high usage of devices

8. I often experience physical tension in my body such as headaches, back pain or neck strain

9. I find it difficult to focus on simple day-to-day tasks

10. I often feel anxious and have the need to check my devices such as smartphones, tabs and laptops frequently
